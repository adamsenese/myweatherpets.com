Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about WeatherPets.

WeatherPets is an iOS weather app that uses AI to place your real pet into beautiful weather-themed scenes. You get all the weather information you need — hourly forecasts, 10-day outlooks, severe weather alerts — presented alongside AI-generated images of your pet reacting to current conditions.
When you add a pet photo, our AI analyzes your pet's appearance — breed, coloring, and unique features. It then generates new scenes showing your pet in weather-appropriate settings. The scenes update based on real-time weather conditions at your location, so your pet is always dressed for the weather.
WeatherPets provides comprehensive weather data powered by Apple WeatherKit, including current conditions, hourly forecasts, 10-day outlooks, severe weather alerts, UV index, wind speed, humidity, precipitation probability, and more. You get the same quality weather data as any top weather app.
Yes. Your pet photos are stored securely using Firebase Cloud Storage with encryption. Photos are used solely for generating AI scenes and are never shared with third parties for advertising or unrelated purposes. You can delete your pet's data at any time from within the app. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
The free version includes basic weather data, one pet profile, and a limited number of AI-generated scenes. Premium unlocks unlimited AI scene generation, multiple pet profiles, style pack credits, home screen widgets, smart notifications with personality, and priority scene generation. Premium is available as a monthly or annual subscription.
Style packs let you customize the artistic style of your pet's weather scenes. Premium subscribers receive style pack credits that can be used to unlock different visual themes — like watercolor, pixel art, or studio photography styles. Credits refresh with your subscription cycle.
WeatherPets is available for iPhone running iOS 17 or later. The app supports all iPhone models compatible with iOS 17, including home screen widgets. An iPad version may be available in the future.
Subscriptions are managed through Apple. To change or cancel your subscription, go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone and select WeatherPets. You can also manage subscriptions through the App Store. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.
WeatherPets sends two types of notifications: morning weather briefings (delivered at your preferred time with a summary from your pet) and severe weather alerts (immediate notifications when dangerous conditions are detected in your area). You can customize notification preferences in the app settings.
You can reach our support team through our support page. We typically respond within 24 hours. You can also find us through the "Contact Support" option in the app settings.
